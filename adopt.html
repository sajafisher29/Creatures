Adoptable Creatures

We offer a diverse yet intimate selection of potential companions. Due to the nature of large scale habitat and resources necessary to provide a comfortable living for our creatures, it is necessary to limit our focus to less than ten beasts within our facilities at a given time. If you decide to apply please submit a brief message including your name, desired foster pet name, and a contact address and we will forward your application. Our application process is quite intensive and approval can take upwards of three weeks. We appreciate your patience.
